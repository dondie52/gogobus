## Account & Registration

### How do I create an account?

1. Visit https://gogobus.co.bw
2. Click "Get Started" or "Sign Up"
3. Enter your email and password
4. Verify your email address
5. Complete your profile with personal information

### I didn't receive the verification email

**Check the following:**
1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Verify email address is correct
3. Wait a few minutes (email may be delayed)
4. Request a new verification email

**Still not working?**
- Contact support: support@gogobus.co.bw
- Provide your email address for assistance

### I forgot my password

1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for password reset link
5. Follow the link to create a new password

### Can I use social login?

Yes! You can sign in with:
- Google
- Facebook
- Apple

Click the social login button on the signup/login page.

## Booking & Payments

### How do I book a ticket?

1. **Search for a trip:**
   - Enter origin and destination
   - Select travel date
   - Click "Search"

2. **Select a bus:**
   - View available buses
   - Choose departure time
   - Click "Select Seats"

3. **Choose seats:**
   - Select your preferred seats
   - Click "Continue"

4. **Enter passenger details:**
   - Fill in passenger information
   - Click "Continue"

5. **Review and pay:**
   - Review booking summary
   - Select payment method
   - Complete payment

6. **Receive ticket:**
   - Get confirmation email
   - Receive QR code ticket

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept:
- **Mobile Money:**
  - Orange Money
  - Mascom MyZaka
  - BTC Smega
- **Cards:** Credit/Debit cards via DPO Pay
- **Cash:** At bus station (if available)

### My payment failed

**Common reasons:**
1. Insufficient funds
2. Network connectivity issues
3. Payment gateway timeout

**What to do:**
1. Wait a few minutes and try again
2. Check your account balance
3. Try a different payment method
4. Contact your payment provider

**Still having issues?**
- Contact support: support@gogobus.co.bw
- Include booking reference if available

### How long does payment confirmation take?

- **Mobile Money:** 1-5 minutes
- **Cards:** Usually instant, up to 5 minutes
- **Cash:** Confirmed at bus station

If payment is not confirmed within 10 minutes, contact support.

### Can I book multiple tickets at once?

Yes! You can:
- Select multiple seats
- Enter passenger details for each seat
- Pay for all tickets in one transaction

Maximum seats per booking: Usually 10 (varies by bus operator).

## Tickets & Travel

### Where is my ticket?

After successful payment:
1. Check your email for confirmation
2. Log in to your account
3. Go to "My Tickets"
4. Your ticket will be displayed with QR code

### How do I use my QR ticket?

1. **At the bus station:**
   - Show QR code to conductor
   - QR code will be scanned
   - Board the bus

2. **Digital ticket:**
   - Open ticket on your phone
   - Ensure QR code is clear
   - Have valid ID ready

### I lost my ticket

**If you're logged in:**
1. Go to "My Tickets"
2. Find your booking
3. View/download ticket

**If you can't access your account:**
- Contact support: support@gogobus.co.bw
- Provide booking reference or email
- We'll resend your ticket

### Can I change my seat after booking?

Seat changes are subject to availability:
1. Go to "My Tickets"
2. Find your booking
3. Check if seat change is available
4. Contact support if needed

**Note:** Seat changes may incur additional fees.

### Can I book for someone else?

Yes! When booking:
1. Select seats for all passengers
2. Enter passenger details for each seat
3. You can use different passenger names

**Note:** Passenger names must match ID shown at boarding.

## Cancellations & Refunds

### How do I cancel my booking?

1. Go to "My Tickets"
2. Find your booking
3. Click "Cancel" or "Request Cancellation"
4. Follow cancellation process
5. Confirmation will be sent via email

### What is the cancellation policy?

**General Policy:**
- **More than 24 hours before departure:** Full refund (minus service fee)
- **Less than 24 hours before departure:** Refund subject to operator policy
- **After departure:** No refund

**Note:** Policies may vary by operator. Check booking confirmation for specific terms.

### How long does a refund take?

- **Processing time:** 3-5 business days
- **To your account:** 5-10 business days (varies by payment method)
- **Total:** 8-15 business days

### How will I receive my refund?

Refunds are issued to the original payment method:
- **Mobile Money:** To your mobile money account
- **Card:** To the card used for payment
- **Cash:** Via bank transfer or mobile money

### Can I change my travel date?

Date changes are subject to:
1. Availability on new date
2. Operator policy
3. Additional fees may apply

**How to request:**
1. Go to "My Tickets"
2. Find your booking
3. Click "Change Date" or contact support

## Technical Support

### The website/app is not loading

**Try the following:**
1. Refresh the page
2. Clear browser cache
3. Try a different browser
4. Check your internet connection
5. Disable browser extensions

**Still not working?**
- Check our status page (if available)
- Contact support with:
  - Browser and version
  - Error message (if any)
  - Screenshot of issue

### I'm getting an error message

**Common errors:**
- **"Payment failed":** Check payment method and try again
- **"Booking not found":** Verify booking reference or contact support
- **"Session expired":** Log out and log back in
- **"Network error":** Check internet connection

**If error persists:**
- Note the exact error message
- Take a screenshot
- Contact support with details

### I can't log in to my account

**Check the following:**
1. Verify email and password are correct
2. Check if Caps Lock is on
3. Try "Forgot Password" to reset
4. Clear browser cache and cookies

**Still can't log in?**
- Contact support: support@gogobus.co.bw
- Provide your email address
- We'll help reset your account

### The QR code is not scanning

**Possible reasons:**
1. QR code not clear (zoom in)
2. Screen brightness too low
3. Damage to QR code

**Solutions:**
1. Download/print ticket
2. Take screenshot and zoom in
3. Request new ticket from support

## General Questions

### Where do I board the bus?

Boarding location is shown on your ticket:
1. Check ticket for boarding location
2. Arrive 30 minutes before departure
3. Look for GOGOBUS signage or your bus operator

### What should I bring?

**Required:**
- Valid ID (National ID, Passport, or Driver's License)
- QR code ticket (digital or printed)
- Booking confirmation

**Recommended:**
- Water and snacks
- Phone charger
- Comfortable clothing

### Can I take luggage?

Yes, with restrictions:
- **Small luggage:** Usually free (under 15kg)
- **Large luggage:** May incur additional fees
- **Restrictions:** Check with operator

**Note:** Check specific operator policies on ticket.

### What if the bus is delayed?

1. Check ticket for bus contact information
2. Contact operator directly
3. Contact support: support@gogobus.co.bw
4. Check our website/app for updates

### Can I get a receipt?

Yes! Receipts are available:
1. Go to "My Tickets"
2. Find your booking
3. Click "Download Receipt" or "Print Receipt"

Receipts are also sent via email after booking.
